What I do?

Here are some of my expertise

Creative Sketching

"Bringing imagination to life through black and white sketches with hatching techniques. Every detail reflects visual sensitivity and a sharp mastery of composition."

Creative Writing

"Creating stories full of emotion and vivid characters. From lyrical poems to short stories, every word is carefully chosen hoping to transport the reader into an imaginative world."

Concept Art

"Creating visual concepts for various stories or artworks, combining traditional and modern ideas. Bringing imaginative visualizations to life with a unique and original approach."

Black and White Art

"mastered enough in black and white to create meaningful works, focusing on contrast, shadow, and deep gradation of shading."
